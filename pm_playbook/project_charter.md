# Project Charter: Q3 VIP Customer Retention Campaign

## 1. Project Background & Business Case
Our data analytics engine revealed a critical revenue vulnerability: **22% of our high-monetary 'VIP / Champion' users have not made a purchase in over 60 days.** This indicates a major churn risk. It is significantly more cost-effective to retain existing high-value customers than to acquire new ones. 

## 2. Project Objectives
* **Primary Goal:** Re-engage at least 15% of the identified "At Risk VIP" customer segment within a 6-week timeframe.
* **Secondary Goal:** Establish an automated, repeatable pipeline that flags at-risk users and pushes them to marketing automation sequences.

## 3. Project Scope
* **In-Scope:** * Automating data segmentation pipeline scripts.
    * Designing targeted email copy and bespoke UI layout variants for the VIP segment.
    * Deploying standard behavioral tracking to monitor click-through and conversion rates.
* **Out-of-Scope:** * Modifying core product pricing structures or offering custom discounts greater than 20%.
    * Re-platforming the underlying CRM system.

## 4. Cross-Functional Team Roles
* **Technical Project Manager (PM):** Coordinates sprints, maintains the product backlog, mitigates development blockages, and manages stakeholder updates.
* **Data Analyst:** Oversees data integrity, updates segmentation logic, and compiles campaign performance reports.
* **UI/UX Designer:** Creates personalized marketing assets and landing page experiences tailored to premium user expectations.
* **Software Engineer:** Configures database attributes to store segmentation flags and integrates automated email trigger webhooks.
